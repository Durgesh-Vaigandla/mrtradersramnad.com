The Year It Began

Roots in Ramanathapuram

What began as an independent local trading post has evolved into the region's largest dedicated paint atelier. We recognized early on that a house becomes a home only when it reflects the personality of those living within it. Since our inception, we have painstakingly built a reputation not just as sellers of paint, but as curators of color and longevity.

The Kilakarai Expansion

Our commitment to serving the architectural needs of the entire district led to the establishment of our Kilakarai branch. This allowed us to bring premium brands and expert consulting closer to coastal developments, ensuring that properties facing unique weather challenges received the specialized exterior coatings they necessitated.
